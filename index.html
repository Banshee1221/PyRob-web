PYTEACHER

Computer Science Concepts

PyTeacher is a software package, a video game, that aims to make introductory computer science easier and more fun to learn. Education is the core purpose and this means posing levels that encourage players to think critically and programmatically, as well as use their knowledge of computer science concepts to progress.

Education that feels Engaging

Video games are fun, addictive and engaging. Traditional education is not always. By using the concept of gaming and applying it to a traditional introductory computer science course, PyTeacher aims to provide an exciting complementary platform to this course allowing students to explore concepts and ideas at their own pace and very visually.

The Familiar Python Syntax

Python is a programming language designed to be simple and easy to learn, while still being powerful and flexible. It has proven itself a useful tool to educate first time computer science students, and as such is the core of what PyTeacher is based around. Players use Python syntax to interact with the game.
